2011 A General Purpose Pipeline for Brain Decoding and Neurofeedback Studies

Together with colleagues at Donders, I developed the real-time functional magnetic resonance imagining pipeline to retrieve, preprocess, and decode fMRI brain scans in real time for neurofeedback studies.
